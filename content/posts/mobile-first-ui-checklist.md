Mobile-first design means prioritizing the smallest screen constraints first.

## Content hierarchy

Ask what matters most:

- headline
- supporting copy
- one clear call-to-action

If all three are obvious on a phone, desktop will be easier.

## Spacing rhythm

Use consistent vertical spacing so sections breathe. Crowded screens feel slower and harder to read.

## Interaction details

Keep touch targets comfortably large, avoid accidental taps, and use subtle hover/focus states for polish.

## Checklist summary

- clear hierarchy
- readable type scale
- comfortable spacing
- simple navigation
- fast-loading media
